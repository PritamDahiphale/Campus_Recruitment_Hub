About the College

The Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology is an autonomous institute of engineering in Pune,India. Established in 2002, it is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. The college is run by the Bansilal Ramanath Agarwal Charitable Trust.In 2017 the University Grant Commission granted it autonomous status.It's consistently ranked as one of the top colleges in Pune,with the recognition and approval of the All India Council of Technical Education, New Delhi. DsP has been accredited by NAAC and has received an A grade with CGPA of 3.14.In 2016,DsP awarded as 'Outstanding Engineering Institute- West" by Vijayavani National Education Leadership Awards.
